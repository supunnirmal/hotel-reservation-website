About Us
Our History

We started our journey in 2010, drawing inspiration from our country’s most mesmerizing beach wich surrounds the island. Just like its pleasant sight, we infuse a rejuvenating spirit into every experience the world discovers with us. Today, with each property we present a new Sri Lanka that houses a contemporary outlook and a genial atmosphere. Together, they put us on the world map beyond the boundaries of hospitality. They make us a group passionate about living the true essence of each moment.

Mission

The mission of the Lotus Hotel is to put hospitality services on the highest level in order to satisfy the demands and expectations of guests

Vision

The ideology of our vision is to continue to apply and set the highest standards of service quality and in that way justify and uphold the reputation that we have among the guests, partners, competitors and the wider community. We use and constantly introduce environmentally friendly technologies and processes in order to remain in balance with nature and also meet the needs of contemporary society.Tradition is a testament to our success but in the future we also want to embrace the changes that modern time brings and become more attractive in the market and more interesting to our guests and partners.

Royal Facilities

Who are in extremely love with eco friendly system.

Restaurant

The main restaurant at Hotel Royal is situated in the center of the hotel. We take after the boundless ocean that surrounds us. So, we’re open 24 hours, serving buffet at breakfast, lunch and dinner and an à la carte at all other times. We dish out Sri Lankan cuisine spiked with regional spices alongside an elaborate continental affair.

Nature Trails

We’re blessed to have many of our resorts adjacent to Sri Lanka’s top national parks, hence we drive for less than 20 minutes from our hotels to celebrate what’s ours and wild in our environment.Curated experiences and magical encounters, are that much special when you are guided by a nature lover.

Swimming Pool

800 square feet callosal pool will provide guests with its mesmerizing mosaic of yellow, white, and blue tiles with perfect lighting at night overviewing the mesmerizing ocean view.

Rent a Car

Hotel provides the convinent facility to rent a vehicle for the guest which encourages the siteseeing in this gorgeous Island.This facility with be provided 24hours and and charged in a hourly rate.

Gymnasium

Offers a wide range of Life Fitness cardio and strength machines, complete with flat screen televisions, designed to help you keep going for a day of meetings or sightseeing. Our extended hotel gym hours fit your schedule, whether you are up before the sun or a night owl.

Spa

What you need of a revitalizing hot stone massage, a soothing body wrap, or an aromatherapy facial. Or all of the above. Our full-service spa aim to please, utilizing eco-friendly products and customized packages to comfort your mind, body, and soul.
